The benefits of accepting card payments.

They’re in line with customer preferences

With cash usage in decline, consumers are used to paying for things quickly and efficiently with their card, or digital wallet. By only accepting card payments, you could be excluding a whole group of customers. But switching to card payments you can unlock convenience for customers and improve their overall experience. 

They could save you time and money

The less time you spend counting loose change, or cashing up at the end of the day, the more time you can spend on things that matter – like engaging with customers, or making growth plans for the future. Dojo card machines make things seamless. From speedy payments to quick data insights to EPOS integration, we’re giving you the tech to make things quicker and more accurate. 

They could reduce on-premise theft 

The less physical money you keep in your premise, the less likely you are to encounter theft. By accepting digital payments with Dojo, you’ll receive your funds in your bank account from 10am the next day –all of the perks of next-day funds, none of the risks that come with it.

Take card payments your way

Different businesses need different solutions. If you’re a restaurant, you’ll want lightweight wireless card machines that can split the bill in seconds. If you’re an intimate retailer, you may need a robust countertop card machine to keep your till company. Or, if you’re up and down the country trading from place to place day to day, you’ll want a fully mobile PDQ machine. 

We’ve got a full range of card machines tailored to your needs. 

The Dojo Go is our portable and fully mobile card machine. It’s a modern, sleek Android machine built with point-to-point encryption – the best security on the market. Not only does that keep your customers’ card data safe, but it makes your PCI compliance a breeze. 

And with an intuitive interface, built-in thermal printer and the ability to split the bill in custom or pre-filled amounts, it works to remove your workload.

The Dojo One is our countertop card machine designed for fixed payments in one place. Rather than using Wi-Fi or 4G, it’s tethered to your till with your Ethernet cable, so it’s reliable and sturdy.
