Secure transaction fee* (per transaction) €0.05

The Secure transaction fee covers the enhanced protection we offer your business. It helps bolster payment security with added safety measures every time you process a transaction. Our best-in-class fraud detection algorithm reduces fraud losses by an average of 45%. Point-to-point encryption for in-person payments provides significantly reduced financial exposure from a data breach. Plus, reporting your PCI compliance takes minutes.

FAQs

Dojo is a payments provider that helps independent businesses accept payments, trade securely and get paid fast. Whether you trade in person, through a website, or over the phone, our goal is to make the everyday simpler. 

If you're interested in joining Dojo, simply fill in the form on this page. Our friendly team usually responds within an hour of details being submitted. If you submit your details during a weekend or bank holiday, we'll get in touch the next working day.

Dojo Go is faster than comparable card machines in the UK, based on research by Paymentsense Limited, trading as Dojo in 2021.

The length of your contract depends on your monthly turnover. We offer a monthly rolling contract for businesses earning €150,000+ per year. For businesses earning under €150,000 per year, our contracts are 6 months and then flip to monthly rolling. Switch to us from another provider and we could pay your cancellation fees up to €3,000.

We transfer your previous day’s takings to your bank account from 10am. The time it takes to reach your account will depend on your bank and how they process transfers.

If your bank handles transfers 7 days a week, you should get your money the same day – even at weekends and bank holidays. If your bank handles transfers 5 days a week (Monday to Friday) then you should get your money the next working day – excluding weekends and bank holidays.

We securely ship your card machine using next working-day delivery.

* In an independent research study with Savanta 2023 in the UK, Dojo could take payments (from key in to authorisation) 58% faster than the market average.
