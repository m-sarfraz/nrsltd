Secure transaction fee* (per transaction) €0.05

The Secure transaction fee covers the enhanced protection we offer your business. It helps bolster payment security with added safety measures every time you process a transaction. Our best-in-class fraud detection algorithm reduces fraud losses by an average of 45%. Point-to-point encryption for in-person payments provides significantly reduced financial exposure from a data breach. Plus, reporting your PCI compliance takes minutes.

Minimum monthly payment charge €24.95

We’ll only charge you this if the total of your transaction fees during the month is under €24.95. The minimum monthly payment charge applies even if you don’t take any card payments throughout the month.
