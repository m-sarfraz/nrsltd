Take fast payments with Dojo

From next-day transfers to 7-day tech support, here are some of the things you can expect when you join Dojo.

Sign-up process

Sign up today and you can be taking payments tomorrow.

  • Speedy sign-up process 

    All we need is a few details about your business. Once you get your machine, you're set up & ready to take payments in less than 5 minutes.

  • Current contract buyout 

    We’ll pay your exit fees with your current provider up to £3,000*.

  • Easy PCI compliance 

    Become PCI compliant in minutes – all it takes is a couple of documents & a few quick steps, all done on the app.*

    *Note that, dependent upon your transaction volumes, you may need to perform additional steps as part of your PCI compliance. Please contact your Payments Consultant or Account Manager if you would like to discuss or require further information.

  • Next working-day terminal delivery 

    We securely ship your card machine using next working-day delivery.
